Features

Hatsune Miku is off on another world tour! With brand-new songs to perform and an enormous wardrobe to style in, the possibilities for unique performances are virtually endless. How will you help Miku, Rin, Len, Luka, MEIKO, and KAITO steal the show? Watch your favorite virtual singers take the stage on a desktop, laptop, or Steam Deck, and even stream performances to a TV!

Choose from Over 170 Songs

Play through a myriad of songs from the Nintendo Switch release of Mega Mix, including content from the DLC Song Packs, all in the standard edition of Mega Mix+.

With the Mega Mix+ DLC pack, get access to an additional 72 songs from Hatsune Miku: Project DIVA Future Tone.

Customize with Over 400 Modules

Dress your favorite virtual singers from head to toe with over 400 modules, and customize the accessories of each virtual singer in your group. Then take to the stage and watch your favorite characters perform in your favorite outfits. Modules and accessories can be customized for each song, allowing for abundant personalization!

Plus, with the Item Unlock Key DLC, get access to all modules from the start!

T-Shirt Editor

Design your own T-Shirt for your favorite virtual singer to wear on stage! Draw both the front and back to your liking, and then preview it before debuting it in a performance! A variety of pre-made designs are also included for even more variety.

Custom Playlists

Use the Custom Playlists feature to create your own assortment of songs. Then, press play to roll the music and watch accompanying music videos in a cinematic viewing experience. Any module customizations applied to a song also appear here!

Practice Mode

Rise to the top by practicing songs on any difficulty and with any modifiers. Practice Mode allows you to play without a background music video for extra focus. While playing a song, you can also quickly rewind and retry a specific segment. This allows you to optimize your moment-to-moment executions and become a master at any song.
